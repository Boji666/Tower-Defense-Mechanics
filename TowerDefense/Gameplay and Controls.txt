
Gameplay:


*Enemies will start to spawn when you deploy all your turrets, until the enemies eat all crystals on the board.



Controls:


Camera:

-Use WASD keys to move the camera.

-Use the scroll button to make zomm in/out

-Use sapce key to reset the camera to default position.

-Use right mouse button to rotate the camera.


Player:

-Use Left mouse button to deploy turrets on the board.

-Use right mouse button over a turret to upgrade to the next level. (Need coins and full upgrade bar).
